Leading the Way to a
Healthier Tomorrow

Welcome to the official website of the MEDICAL CENTER of TANDANG SORA — your trusted partner in community health and wellness. We are committed to providing affordable, accessible, and compassionate healthcare services to all members of our community. From preventive care to essential medical services, and maintenance, our goal is to support you at every stage of life.

The Municipal of Tandang Sora, City of Quezon is committed to promoting the health and well-being of every citizen. Through the Fifth Medical Center, we provide accessible, affordable, and compassionate healthcare services rooted in dignity and respect.

We go beyond clinical care—empowering communities through outreach, education, and wellness programs. With skilled professionals and modern facilities, we ensure quality treatment for all, including new patients, returning residents, and referring physicians.

At the heart of our mission is a healthier, empowered Tandang Sora for generations to come.

To build a balanced and empowered community where every resident of Tandang Sora has access to quality and essential health services.
